Work Order Types
1.	Preventive Maintenance (PM): calendar/usage-based servicing, checklists, parts planning
2.	Corrective Maintenance (CM): breakdown repairs, root cause analysis, closure reports
3.	Predictive Maintenance (PdM): condition monitoring, trend analysis, alerts
4.	Emergency Work Orders: life safety-critical tasks, rapid response, escalation
5.	Inspection Work Orders: periodic compliance checks, statutory inspections
6.	Calibration Work Orders: instruments recalibration with certificates
7.	Small Projects/Minor Works: renovations, fit-outs, moves/adds/changes (MAC)
8.	Soft Services: cleaning, landscaping, pest control, waste management


LOCATION

Recommended hierarchy: Site → Complex → Building → Floor → Zone → Room → Asset

•	Site: geographic site name, address, climate zone
•	Campus: multi-building grouping (e.g., Business Park)
•	Building: tower/block, gross area, occupancy type
•	Floor: floor index, use type (office, retail, technical)
•	Zone: fire compartment, wing, tenant demarcation
•	Room/Space: space ID, function (e.g., server room, lobby)
•	Asset: unique asset tag, parent system, location reference

Financial:
Goal: How much does it actually cost to keep this specific building or machine running?
Every penny spent must be accounted for and tagged to a specific asset or building.
- Budget: Where the money is coming from
- Expense: Where the money is going to
Purchase request(PR):
    - Warehouse notices stock is low, or technician needs to repair
    - They create a PR in the system
    - Item name, quantity, estimated price, reasoning.
Approval and validation:
- Checks if there's enough in the budget for for PR
- If yes goes to manager/admin for approval
- If no, blocks or flags as over budget
Purchase order(PO):
    - Once approved, converts PR to PO
    - PO is sent to supplier
    - Estimated cost is now "committed in the budget"
    - PO is sent to supplier
    - Supplier sends invoice
    - Invoice is sent to finance
    - Finance approves invoice
    - Invoice is sent to warehouse
    - Warehouse receives materials
    - Warehouse updates stock
    - Warehouse updates movement logs
